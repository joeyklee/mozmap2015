Navigate the chaos of MozFest!

Here's how it works:

  • The map is organized by space (from top to bottom) and time (from left to right).
  • Hover over each dot to reveal more information about each session.
  • You can follow one of the many pathways for a more curated MozFest experience or simply choose you own adventure.
  • Tweet #mozmap + session number (e.g. #mozmap12) to check-in to your session.
